How Easy Bloods Works

Taking control of your health has never been simpler. At Easy Bloods, we've streamlined the process of blood testing to be convenient, reliable, and accessible from the comfort of your own home. Here's a step-by-step guide to how it works:

1. Select Your Test Online

Browse our comprehensive range of blood tests online. Choose the test that suits your health needs and add it to your basket. Our clear descriptions and easy-to-navigate website make selecting the right test straightforward.

2. Receive Your Easy-to-Use Kit

Once your order is confirmed, we'll promptly dispatch your discreetly packaged blood collection kit directly to your address. The kit contains everything you need to collect your sample, along with clear and simple instructions.

3. Collect Your Sample Comfortably at Home

Following the step-by-step instructions provided in your kit, you can easily collect your small blood sample in the comfort and privacy of your own home at a time that suits you best. Our lancets are designed for minimal discomfort.

4. Return Your Sample Securely

Once you've collected your sample, simply package it as instructed and use the pre-paid, tracked postage label included in your kit to return it to our accredited laboratory. You can have peace of mind knowing your sample is handled securely.

5. Expert Laboratory Analysis

Your sample will be analysed by experienced technicians in our certified laboratory using the latest technology and stringent quality control measures. This ensures accurate and reliable results you can trust.

6. Receive Your Confidential Results Online

Once your analysis is complete, your results will be securely uploaded to your private online account. You can access your results at any time, from any device, ensuring complete confidentiality and convenience.

7. Understand Your Results & Next Steps

Your results will be presented in a clear and easy-to-understand format. Should you have any questions or wish to discuss your results further, our customer support team is available to provide guidance and support.
